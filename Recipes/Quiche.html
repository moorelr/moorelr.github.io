Quiche

Rev. 3/7/2022 LRM.

Searched specifically for a quiche recipe that uses a lot of eggs. Turned out great!
Modified from https://www.spendwithpennies.com/easy-quiche-recipe/

Ingredients

Pie crust:

  • Flour -- 1 cup
  • Butter -- 1/3 cup
  • Water -- just enough to come together

Filling:

  • Eggs -- 6 random-sized eggs
  • Milk -- 3/4 cup
  • Salt -- 3/4 tsp
  • Black pepper -- 1/4 tsp
  • Ham -- 1 cup chopped, cooked ham
  • Cheese -- 1.5 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided (6 oz)
  • Green onion -- 3 tbsp green onion, crushed and thinly-sliced (2 big stalks or 3-4 small stalks)

Instructions

  1. Prepare pie crust
  2. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  3. Unroll pie crust and press into a 9" pie plate, crimping the top edges if desired.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt and pepper.
  5. Sprinkle ham, 1 cup of cheese, and green onions into the pie crust and pour the egg mixture over top. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup cheese on top of egg mixture.
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes until the center is completely set (38 minutes in our oven). Let cool for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.
