Frozen burritos

Rev. 5/29/2022 LRM.

At the time of writing, these frozen burritos account for ~25% of my diet. Learning how to make these has been life-changing... hopefully for the better!

Ingredients

  • Eggs -- 6 eggs, beaten
  • Meat -- 1 lb of ground hot Italian sausage (or similar)
  • Rice -- 2 c. dry
  • Beans -- ~16 oz can of black beans
  • Pepper -- 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • Onion -- 1 medium onion, diced
  • Cheese -- 4 oz cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Tortillas -- 3 packages of medium flour tortillas

Also, make sure to have these available:

  • Wax paper
  • Masking tape
  • Cooking oil
  • Potato masher

Instructions

  1. Cook beans if necessary: boil hard 1 minute, cover and let stand for 1 hour, simmer until done, and set aside
  2. Cook rice, let stand for ~1 hour partially covered, and add to mixing bowl.
  3. Scramble eggs, and set aside in a large mixing bowl.
  4. Cook and break up sausage in skillet, and add to mixing bowl. Pro-tip: use a potato masher to break the meat into small pieces.
  5. Sautee bell pepper and onion in skillet, and add to mixing bowl.
  6. Mix ingredients thoroughly.
  7. Assemble burritos (~1/2 cup of filling each)
  8. Wrap in wax paper, seal with masking tape, and freeze.

To cook from frozen:

  1. Wrap burrito in a paper towel, and heat in microwave for 1 minute at full power.
  2. Flip burrito over, poke in the center with a fork to create a vent, and cook at full power for another minute.
  3. Flip and cook for additional time if necessary.
  4. Serve hot and expediently.
