Baked Bean Dip

Rev. 9/27/2021 LRM.

Learned this recipe from a friend from the rescue squad.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese -- 2 blocks
  • Canned Chili -- 2 cans of hornet turkey chili with beans
  • Cheddar cheese -- 2 cups, shredded
  • Salsa -- 1/2 cup
  • Pickled jalapenos -- 1/4 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Spread cream cheese on the bottom of a 9x13 glass baking dish, and add canned chili.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes, then remove from oven
  4. Top with cheese and jalapenos, and bake for 5 more minutes
  5. Top with salsa, and serve (or cover with Al foil and baking towels to transport).
