Chocolate chip cookies

Rev. 5/23/2022 LRM.

This is a great recipe, but for some reason I never add the correct amount of flour. Someties it's because of being inebriated. Unfortunately the source was lost, but it was probably the top-rated AllRecipes take on chocolate chip cookies at the time.

Ingredients

  • Butter -- 2 sticks (microwave 30 seconds)
  • White sugar -- 1 c.
  • Brown sugar -- 1 c. packed
  • Eggs -- 2 large eggs
  • Vanilla extract -- 2 tsp.
  • Baking soda -- 1 tsp.
  • Salt -- 1/2 tsp.
  • Flour -- ~3 c.
  • Chocolate chips -- 1 c., semisweet
  • Hot water -- 2 tsp.

Other stuff to have on hand:

  • Parchment paper
  • Cooling racks
  • Baking sheet

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time.
  4. Add vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda and salt in hot water, then stir in.
  6. Add flour, and mix thoroughly.
  7. Add chocolate chips.
  8. Spoon dough balls onto baking sheet.
  9. Bake ~10 minutes or until done, and rest on cooling rack.
