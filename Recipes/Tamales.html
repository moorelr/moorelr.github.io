Tamales

Rev. 8/12/2023 LRM.

Trying to develop an easy, baby-friendly tamale recipe. Definitely a work in progress...
Modified from https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/34512/real-homemade-tamales/
and https://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/easy-tamales/

Ingredients

Wrapping

  • Corn husks
  • Masa flour (instant) -- 2 cups
  • Vegetable oil -- 1/4 cup (or lard or bacon fat)
  • Chicken stock -- 2 cups
  • Baking powder -- 1/2 tsp
  • Cumin -- 1 tsp
  • Salt -- 1 tsp

Filling:

  • Filling -- 1 c (meat, beans, cheese, etc.)
  • Enchilada sauce -- 4 oz

Instructions

Assembly

  1. Soak corn husks in warm water (from the sink) for 30 minutes
  2. Assemble filling in a bowl, and mix together.
  3. Mix dry ingredients.
  4. Mix in oil to dry ingredients, and then gradually add chicken stock.
  5. Flatten out corn husk, pat down a pancake of masa dough, add about 1/4 cup of filling.
  6. Fold tamale closed.

Cooking

  • Option 1: Steam tamales in a steamer basket on stove top for 30 minutes. Serve hot!
  • Option 2: Freeze tamales uncooked, then steam as above.
  • Option 3: Freeze tamales cooked (steam as described above), then reheat in the microwave.
